Akan Birth Names

"Let's go 🇬🇭Ghana with Akan Birth Names"

Akan Birth Names are derived from Ghanian culture.In Ghana, children are given their first name as a "day name" which corresponds to the day of the week they were born. This web application takes a user's Birth Day and calculates the day of the week the user was born, depending on your gender it outputs your Akan Name. The user must select his/her birth day of the week,month and year he was born the lastly seclect the gender and submit via submission button which will thereafter display your Akan Name.
