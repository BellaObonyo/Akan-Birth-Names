Our Birth Names

Brief description about the project

This web application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they

were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.


Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given

their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they

were born. Here are Ghanian day names.


The system takes in your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and
depending on your gender, it displays/outputs your Akan Name.
For this to work, one must select his/her birth day of the week,month and year hewas born the lastly seclect the gender and submit via submission button which will prompt a feedback display of your Akan Name
